COVID-19 Self-Assessment

Do you have any of these symptoms?

  • Fever and/or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss or change of sense of smell or taste
  • Muscle aches or joint pain, for example, aches or pains in your body that you don't normally have
  • Extreme tiredness
  • Sore throat
  • Runny or stuffy/congested nose
  • Headache
  • Nausea, vomiting and/or diarrhea
  • Abdominal pain
  • Pink eye
  • Decreased or no appetite (young children only)
